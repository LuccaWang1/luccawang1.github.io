About

I'm a software developer with experience bridging data and business needs. In my current role at a global insurance brokerage, I collaborate with U.S. and international accounting teams to turn complex financial data into actionable insights and support a global standardization initiative focused on improving data consistency and reporting. My tech toolkit includes SQL, Oracle Fusion Cloud, Azure, Python, JavaScript, React, and VSCode.

Previously, I worked as a full-stack developer building applications with Python, Django, React, and AWS. I improved code maintainability, user experience, and system performance, while gaining hands-on experience with Docker, AWS ECS/EC2, Postman, MySQL, and accessibility standards.

I've also built personal projects using Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, and various front-end libraries and APIs. With a foundation in computer science fundamentals and a Software Engineering certificate from Hackbright Academy, I'm a lifelong learner driven by problem solving, clear communication, and thoughtful design.

Outside of coding, I'm a marathon finisher and puppy mom - reminders that patience and perseverance are key to growth in life and software. Thanks for visiting my website, https://luccawang.dev, that I hand built and maintain!
