About

As a full-stack software engineer, I develop apps with Python, JavaScript (ES6), React, and HTML plus CSS, using Postgres, SQL, and APIs. I'm familiar with the CS fundamentals and industry tools, including Code, Git, CL, GitHub, and more. I am a full-time Software Engineering program graduate of Hackbright Academy and am a self-taught developer.

After graduating with 3 undergraduate degrees in journalism and mass communication and visual art in 5 years, I started a career in the nonprofit sector, helping people in communities locally, regionally, and nationally for over a decade.

While my former career as a communications professional was rewarding, I found myself increasingly diving into technology, especially surrounding websites, databases, and emails. I transitioned into software engineering, because I enjoy making things and want to use my creative thinking and strategic problem solving skills to continue building meaningful products.
